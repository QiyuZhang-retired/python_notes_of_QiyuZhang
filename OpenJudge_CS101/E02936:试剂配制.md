# E02936:试剂配制
**算法核心：条件语句**
```python
N=int(input())
nums=list(map(int,input().split()))
if 1 in nums and 2 in nums:
    print(0)
elif 3 in nums and 4 in nums:
    print(0)
elif (5 in nums and 6 not in nums)or(5 not in nums and 6 in nums ):
    print(0)
elif 7 not in nums and 8 not in nums:
    print(0)
else:
    print(1)
```
