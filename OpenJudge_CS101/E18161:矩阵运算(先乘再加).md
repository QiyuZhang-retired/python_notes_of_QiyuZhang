# E18161:矩阵运算(先乘再加)
**算法核心：矩阵**
```python
A=[]
row1,col1=map(int,input().split())
for _ in range(row1):
    A.append(list(map(int,input().split())))
B=[]
row2,col2=map(int,input().split())
for _ in range(row2):
    B.append(list(map(int,input().split())))
C=[]
row3,col3=map(int,input().split())
for _ in range(row3):
    C.append(list(map(int,input().split())))

#判断能否运算
if col1!=row2 or row1!=row3 or col2!=col3:
    print('Error!')
    exit()

#进行运算
D=[[0]*col2 for _ in range(row1)]
for i in range(row1):
    for j in range(col2):
        for k in range(col1):
            D[i][j]+=A[i][k]*B[k][j]
        D[i][j]+=C[i][j]

for d in D:
    print(' '.join(list(map(str,d))))
```
