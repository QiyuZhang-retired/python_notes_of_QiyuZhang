# E19942:二维矩阵上的卷积运算
**算法核心：矩阵**
```python
m,n,p,q=map(int,input().split())
matrix=[]
for _ in range(m):
    matrix.append(list(map(int,input().split())))
core=[]
for _ in range(p):
    core.append(list(map(int,input().split())))

output=[[0]*(n-q+1) for _ in range(m-p+1)]
for i in range(m-p+1):
    for j in range(n-q+1):
        for k in range(p):
            for l in range(q):
                output[i][j]+=core[k][l]*matrix[i+k][j+l]

for row in output:
    print(' '.join(list(map(str,row))))
```
