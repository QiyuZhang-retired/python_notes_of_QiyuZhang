# E04146:数字方格
**算法核心：for循环**  
n较小，直接三重循环
```python
n=int(input())
nums=range(n+1)

S=[]
for a1 in nums:
    for a2 in nums:
        for a3 in nums:
            if (a1+a2)%2==0 and (a2+a3)%3==0 and (a1+a2+a3)%5==0:
                S.append(a1+a2+a3)

print(max(S))
```
